End the greenwash - Drax court case demonstration

Thursday 10 February

11:00 to 13:00

Leeds Crown Court, 1 Oxford Row, Leeds, GB, LS1 3BG

Get directions...

Drax Power Station has been summoned to court to answer to allegations of endangering hundreds of its workers health by exposing them to dangerous levels of wood dust air pollution.

We must hold Drax to account, and not let this go unnoticed. So join the Axe Drax coalition in a protest outside the court.

If you can't turn up in person, please do still sign up. There will be online actions for you to take part in, that are vital for getting the word out.
